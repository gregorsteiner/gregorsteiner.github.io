## About me

I am a first year PhD student at the University of Warwick. My research interests revolve around causal inference and model uncertainty. Currently, I am working on Bayesian model averaging and model selection in instrumental variable models under the supervision of [Prof. Mark Steel](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/sci/statistics/staff/academic-research/steel/).

Before starting the PhD, I earned an MSc in Economics from WU Vienna and a BSc in Statistics from the University of Vienna. Additionally, I gained valuable experience working at the Centre for Medical Data Science at the Medical University of Vienna, where I developed and maintained statistical software.
